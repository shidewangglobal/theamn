## Suggested Web Mapping

- **Display / Hero headings:** HelveticaNeue 1 or 3 (60-76pt → ~3.75-4.75rem)
- **Section headings:** HelveticaNeue 2 Bold at 30-40pt (1.875-2.5rem)
- **Body text:** HelveticaNeue 2 at 16-18pt (1-1.125rem)
- **UI / captions:** Helvetica Now Display at 12-16pt (0.75-1rem)
